### Next field test plan

**Priority 0 — repeatability baseline (unchanged from prior report).** With CV at 33–41% and the 1.25 m/s noise floor swamping every A/B delta, no parameter sweep is interpretable until variance is characterized. Hold one config fixed, fire 5 consecutive runs, compute stdev. If stdev > 0.5 m/s, debug rig-mechanical causes (projectile seating, capacitor recharge time, gate sensor alignment, breech temperature) before any further tuning.

**Priority 1 — verify the coil_2 sweet spot.** This is now the strongest data-backed parameter signal we have, and the prior report's recommendation pointed in the *wrong* direction. The next sweep should resolve this:

**Design intent:**
- Runs 1–5 turn coil 2 from "least-trustworthy parameter" into a real curve at one fixed delay. After this you'll know whether the top-quartile signal is real and where the optimum sits.
- Runs 6–7 verify or refute the surprising top-quartile finding that delays of 250–500 µs are also competitive.
- All runs ≥3 reps so we can compute per-config stdev and have something to feed back into the noise model.

**De-prioritized from the prior report:** the "test coil_1 below 1090 µs" runs. The top-quartile profile says coil 1 has no signal — extending its sweep further is unlikely to pay off until something else is locked down.
