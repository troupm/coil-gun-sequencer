### Physics interpretation

All three active parameters show **negative** correlations with gate 2 transit and G1→G2 flight velocities. This aligns with back-EMF theory: longer pulse durations and longer inter-coil delays mean the projectile spends more time in the decelerating tail of the magnetic field after passing the coil midpoint. Shorter pulses cut the field before back-EMF dominates.

However, the correlation with muzzle velocity (G2→G3) is nearly zero or slightly positive for some parameters — suggesting that what's optimal for stage 1→2 transit may not be optimal for overall exit velocity. The coil 3 stage timing (`gate_2_coil_3_delay_us`, `coil_3_pulse_duration_us`) has not been varied, so we can't yet optimize the final stage.
